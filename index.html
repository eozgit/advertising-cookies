  • FREE DELIVERY
    Standard Delivery - FREE with every 6 bottles purchase
    • FREE delivery on all orders over 6 bottles of wine, 7 days a week (excluding public holidays)
    • The majority of orders will be delivered within 7 days
    • Add FREE glass and chiller bin hire to your order (deposit required)
    Nominated Day Delivery - FREE on all orders over £150
    • FREE delivery on orders over £150, £6.95 on orders over £30 & £8.95 on orders under £30
    • Nominated Day delivery when you order before delivery cut off excluding public holidays*
    • Don’t wait in! We’ll drop you a text on the morning of your expected delivery and let you know a 2 hour delivery window
    * There are a small number of postcodes, where we cannot offer Free Standard delivery or Nominated Day delivery - please check our delivery page for full details
  • Free Click & Collect
    Click and Collect - FREE on all orders
    • Order online and select the free Click & Collect option to collect from store
    • Place your order and collect within 3-7 days, once you have received an email confirming it is ready to collect (exception stores apply)
    • There's no minimum order, so you can collect as much or as little as you want
  • No Quibble Guarantee
    The Majestic money back guarantee Your money back if your wine's not delicious - no quibble, no questions asked

    If you receive an unsatisfactory bottle, we will always replace, refund, or recommend another wine we think you’ll love – and there is no need to return the bottle to us. If you do not enjoy a bottle you ordered, simply get in touch with your local Majestic Wine store, let them know you did not enjoy this wine and our highly knowledgeable staff will be more than happy to replace the wine with something else we think you’ll love or refund the full amount to you.

    Try something new today!

  • Free Glass Hire
    Our glass hire service includes wine glasses, flutes and hi-ball tumblers.*

    All we ask for is a £1-per-glass deposit, payable in advance when adding glasses to your order
    When the glasses are returned to us clean and ready for re-use, the deposit is fully refunded– all you pay for is breakages.
    And since you have paid a deposit, there's no time limit in which you have to return them.
    You can request glasses when you order online, simply enter how many wine glasses, flutes or hi-ball tumblers you'd like to your trolley, all avalible in multiples of 12.

    *Subject to store availability
  • Free Chiller Bin Hire
    Majestic offers free chiller bin* loan for customers purchasing wine from us. When ordering, a £15 per bin deposit is added to your order. This deposit will be refunded when you return the items to us, clean and ready to re-use.

    Our bins hold 18-24 bottles of wine or Champagne or 2-3 cases of lager when filled with ice and water. Drinks will be chilled in 30-45 minutes and will be kept cold for several hours, especially if kept in the shade.
    We can also sell the ice to go in a chiller bin, or have arrive separately - simply add to your trolley at the time of adding the chiller bins

    *Subject to store availability
